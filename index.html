HOW IT WORKS
Talk. Vibe. Vote. Win.
Koala puts 5 random people in one live video room. After each round, players vote one person out. The last 2 people win.
1
Talk
You enter a live video room with 4 strangers.
2
Vibe
Make people laugh, tell a story, start a debate, or just be yourself.
3
Vote
At the end of each round, everyone votes one person out.
4
Win
Survive until the final 2, gain followers, and become one of the most popular people on Koala.
