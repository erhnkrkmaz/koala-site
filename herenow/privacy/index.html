Spotin Privacy Policy

This Policy explains what Spotin collects, why we collect it, how location check-ins work, and the choices you have.

This Policy is drafted for the current Spotin product design and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before App Store release.

1. Who We Are

Spotin is operated by Koala Tech AI ("Koala Tech AI," "we," "our," or "us"). Spotin helps adults at supported clubs check in, see other recently checked-in users, and connect professionally.

Contact us at info@heykoala.app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide, information needed to operate check-ins, and limited technical information.

  • Account information: Apple Sign in identifier and, if Apple provides it, your name or email address.
  • Profile information: display name, bio, profile photo, LinkedIn URL, profile visibility settings, age confirmation, and terms acceptance time.
  • Photo review information: approved photos, pending photos, review status, rejection status, and related moderation records.
  • Location check-in information: when you tap check in, we receive your device latitude, longitude, accuracy, and timestamp to verify whether you are at a supported club or venue.
  • Check-in records: club or venue, check-in time, expiration time, hidden status, and approximate recency shown to other eligible users.
  • Connections and messages: connection requests, accepted connections, message content, message timestamps, sender and recipient identifiers, and related delivery or thread information.
  • Safety and moderation information: reports, blocks, reported profile details, and information needed to investigate abuse.
  • Technical information: device, app, network, request, authentication, storage, and security logs generated by our app infrastructure and service providers.

3. How Location Works

Spotin uses location only when you ask to check in. We use it to verify that your device appears to be inside the supported club or venue check-in area. We do not show your exact latitude or longitude to other users.

Other checked-in users may see that you checked in at the same club or venue and roughly how recently you checked in. A check-in may remain visible for up to 24 hours unless you hide it earlier.

4. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • create and authenticate your account;
  • create, update, and display your profile;
  • verify club check-ins and prevent remote or fake check-ins;
  • show eligible checked-in users to each other;
  • show your approved profile photo, display name, bio, and LinkedIn URL to eligible checked-in users when you are visible in Spotin;
  • send, receive, store, and display connection requests and messages between users;
  • review profile photos before they appear publicly;
  • process blocks, reports, abuse prevention, and safety moderation;
  • provide support, troubleshoot bugs, secure the service, and prevent fraud;
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

We do not use precise location for advertising. We do not sell personal information.

5. What Other Users Can See

When you are checked in and eligible to appear, other eligible checked-in users at the same supported club or venue may see your display name, bio, approved profile photo or initial, LinkedIn URL, club or venue, and approximate check-in recency.

Your LinkedIn URL is intended to let other eligible users open or view your LinkedIn profile. LinkedIn controls what appears on LinkedIn after someone opens your LinkedIn URL.

Pending or rejected profile photos are not shown to other users. If your photo is under review and you do not already have an approved photo, your profile may show your initial instead. If you already have an approved photo and submit a replacement photo, your existing approved photo may remain visible while the new photo is under review.

If you accept a connection request or another user accepts your request, you and that user may be able to send messages to each other. Message content is visible to the sender and recipient and may be processed by Spotin as described in this Policy.

6. How We Share Information

We may share information with:

  • Service providers that host, authenticate, store, secure, or support the app, including backend and storage providers.
  • Apple when you use Sign in with Apple.
  • Third-party services such as LinkedIn when you choose to open a professional link.
  • Other eligible users as part of the Spotin experience, including your approved profile photo or initial, display name, bio, LinkedIn URL, check-in venue, approximate check-in recency, connection requests, and messages you choose to send.
  • Law enforcement, regulators, or legal parties if required by law or necessary to protect rights, safety, users, or the service.
  • Successors if we are involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not knowingly share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

7. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to operate Spotin, provide your account, maintain safety, comply with law, resolve disputes, and enforce our Terms.

  • Active check-ins are intended to be visible for up to 24 hours unless hidden earlier.
  • Profile data remains while your account exists unless you update or delete it.
  • Connection and message records remain while needed to provide messaging, support, safety, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or account features.
  • Reports, blocks, security logs, and moderation records may be kept longer when needed for safety, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or dispute handling.
  • If you delete your account, we delete or de-identify account data unless retention is reasonably necessary for legal, security, safety, or abuse-prevention reasons.

8. Your Choices

  • Edit profile: you can update your name, bio, LinkedIn URL, and photo in the app.
  • Photo review: new photos may require approval before they appear to others.
  • Hide check-in: you can hide your active check-in at any time.
  • Location permission: you can deny location permission, but you will not be able to check in or view the member list without a valid check-in.
  • LinkedIn: you can update your LinkedIn URL in the app. A valid LinkedIn URL may be required for a complete visible profile, and eligible checked-in users may be able to open it.
  • Connections: you can choose whether to accept or decline connection requests. You can report or block users who misuse requests or messages.
  • Delete account: you can delete your account in the app.

9. California Privacy Notice

California residents may have rights to know, access, delete, correct, opt out of certain sharing, limit certain use of sensitive personal information, and not be discriminated against for exercising privacy rights. These rights may depend on whether the law applies to Koala Tech AI at the time of the request.

You can submit privacy requests by emailing info@heykoala.app. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

10. Children

Spotin is for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at info@heykoala.app.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. International Use

Spotin is operated from the United States. If you use Spotin from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will take reasonable steps to notify you. The "Last updated" date shows when this Policy was last changed.

14. Contact

Questions, deletion requests, privacy requests, or safety concerns can be sent to info@heykoala.app.
